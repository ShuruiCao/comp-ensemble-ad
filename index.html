Trajectory Anomaly Detection with By-Design Complementary Detectors

We propose CETrajAD, an ensemble method that combines complementary detectors to detect diverse and realistic trajectory anomalies. By leveraging three trajectory embeddings—Route, Speed, and Shape—our approach captures various anomaly types based on length, direction, shape, and speed. Through a novel ensemble design, CETrajAD improves anomaly detection performance.
